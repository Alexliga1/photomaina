Photography

Photography is the art and science of capturing light to create still or moving images. The word "photography" comes from the Greek words "photo" meaning "light" and "graphia" meaning "to draw." Photography has been around for nearly two centuries, and has since evolved into a highly sophisticated form of expression and communication.
